Distribution

The smoothness score shows how evenly spaced the motifs are in a genomic region. Regions with regular, uniform spacing get higher scores. It is calculated by sliding a window across the motif distances, measuring local variation using a robust median-based method, and converting it into a score where less variation means higher smoothness.
